Welcome to Guavagrams!

The goal of the game is to play all of your letter tiles as fast as you can.

All of your words must be connected like a cross-word. You may rearrange letters as often as you like.

If you have a letter you can't use, drag it to the 'Dump' to recieve a new letter. You may only do this three times.

When you are done press the 'Done!' button to check your words.

Compete for the high score and have fun!
